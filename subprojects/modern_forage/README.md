# Modern Forage

Field research on American regional food: what to eat, where to eat it, and how to find it before it gets flattened by the corporate machine.

Companion to the writing at [ben.abbitt.me/posts/q/modern_forage](https://ben.abbitt.me/posts/q/modern_forage).

## Directories

- **[dishes/](dishes/README.md)** — 190 hyper-local American dishes that locals eat constantly and outsiders have never heard of.
- **[restaurants/](restaurants/README.md)** — finding small chains in the goldilocks zone: big enough to prove the food's worth traveling for, small enough to still have a soul.
- **[snacks/](snacks/README.md)** — state-by-state inventory of nostalgic regional snacks: chips, candy bars, jerky, and gas-station prepared food.
